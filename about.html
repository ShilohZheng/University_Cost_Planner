About This Project

A Grade 12 Finance/Accounting capstone project focused on university cost planning and financial literacy.

Project Purpose

This website helps students understand the real cost of university, including tuition, housing, food, and personal expenses.

What I Learned

I learned how to design and build a multi-page website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and how to present financial data clearly and effectively.

Real-World Connection

University is one of the biggest financial decisions in life. This project connects classroom learning to real-world financial planning.

My Goal

To help high school students better prepare for university by making financial planning simple, visual, and easy to understand.
